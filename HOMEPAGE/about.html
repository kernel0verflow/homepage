Why I created this Webpage?

Welcome to my personal website. I decided to create this in June 2022. I strongly believe that every person on this planet should have their own website! I have several reasons for this. I am a big privacy advocate and don't like to share my data with big companies. However, this is the case when using social media services.

On a self-designed and hosted website, you have all the freedom. You want to build a chat room or start a blog. All this is possible with the help of a personal page. Or you can create a personal online diary, without the control of large IT corporations.
